SAMPLE 1 — ACCOUNT VERIFICATION PHISHING
Classification: PHISHING (High Risk)

--- Simulated Header ---
Return-Path: <bounce@secure-account-verify.com>
Received: from mail.secure-account-verify.com [45.33.12.88]
SPF: FAIL (domain does not designate 45.33.12.88 as a permitted sender)
DKIM: none
From: Security Team <support@secure-account-verify.com>
Reply-To: alerts@verify-helpdesk-online.com
Subject: ⚠️ Urgent: Your Account Will Be Locked

--- Email Body ---
Dear User,

We noticed suspicious activity on your account.
To avoid account suspension, please verify your details immediately.

Verify Now: http://secure-account-verify[.]com

Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in permanent account lock.

Regards,
Security Team

--- Indicators Identified ---
1. Sender domain unrelated to any real organisation.
2. SPF authentication fails.
3. Urgency / fear-based language ("immediately", "permanent lock", "24 hours").
4. Generic greeting ("Dear User").
5. Defanged link formatting unusual for genuine business email.
